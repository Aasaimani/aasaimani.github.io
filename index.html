Duality

My work focuses on the interface between algebraic structure and analytic behaviour. I study Fourier and Fourier–Stieltjes algebras, representation-theoretic function spaces, and how these analytic objects capture the structural features of locally compact groups.

A central theme in my research is understanding what aspects of a group are preserved or transformed when viewed through its dual spaces.
