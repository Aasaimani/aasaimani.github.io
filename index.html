About

Since October 2024, I have been serving as a Guest Faculty in the Department of Mathematics at Pondicherry University, teaching foundational mathematics courses to engineering students. My academic journey includes two years as a Postdoctoral Fellow at University of Waterloo, Canada, where I also earned my Ph.D. in 2021 under the guidance of Professors Dr. Nico Spronk and Dr. Brian Forrest. Just before that fellowship, I spent a year as a Postdoctoral Fellow at University of Winnipeg, Canada, working with Professors Dr. Ross Stokke and Dr. Matthew Wiersma. I began my research career with an integrated master’s degree from National Institute of Science Education and Research Bhubaneswar, India.
